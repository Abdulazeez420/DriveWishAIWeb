Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

13.1 Overview

DriveWise AI is designed to protect your privacy by default. The app operates entirely offline and does not transmit your vehicle data to any external server owned or operated by us.

13.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (Android only) — displays ads after consent is granted (GDPR compliant). Three ad formats: banner ads on list pages, rewarded ads gating premium exports, and frequency-capped interstitial ads shown at most once every three saves. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Drive (optional) — stores your encrypted backup in your own Google Drive appDataFolder. DriveWise AI never accesses any other files in your Drive. Google Privacy Policy
  • Dropbox (optional) — stores your encrypted backup ZIP in your private /Apps/DriveWise AI folder. DriveWise AI never accesses any other Dropbox files. Dropbox Privacy Policy
  • Google ML Kit (on-device only) — OCR text recognition runs entirely on-device; no images are uploaded. Google Privacy Policy

13.4 Advertising & Consent

We display ads via Google AdMob (Android only). On first launch, you are presented with a GDPR consent dialog. You may decline personalised ads — the app remains fully functional with non-personalised ads shown instead.

Banner ads — shown at the bottom of list pages.
Rewarded ads — premium export features (Tax Deduction Report, Maintenance & Expense PDF/Excel, Export History) show a "Premium Feature" unlock sheet. Tapping Watch Ad to Unlock plays a short rewarded ad; once the reward is earned the export is generated. Closing the sheet without watching cancels the action — no export is triggered.
Interstitial ads — shown at most once every three saves across fuel, maintenance, and expense entries combined.

13.5 Data Export & Deletion

You can export all your data at any time from Privacy & Data → Export My Data in JSON, PDF, or Excel format. Premium exports are gated behind a short rewarded ad via the Premium Unlock sheet; if the ad fails to load the export proceeds immediately as a fallback.

To permanently delete all data, go to Privacy & Data → Wipe Data. This deletes the encrypted database and all secure keys from your device. This action is irreversible.

13.6 Cloud Backup Privacy

If you enable Google Drive backup, your encrypted database is transferred directly between your device and your personal Google Drive appDataFolder. DriveWise AI does not have access to your cloud credentials or the uploaded file contents.
