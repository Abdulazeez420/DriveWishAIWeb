Overview

DriveWise AI is designed to protect your privacy by default. The app operates entirely offline and does not transmit your vehicle data to any external server owned or operated by us.

All vehicle details, fuel logs, maintenance records, trip logs, and expenses are stored locally on your device inside an AES-256 hardware-backed encrypted Isar database. No account is required. No cloud registration. Your data belongs to you.

Third-Party Services

DriveWise AI integrates with the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

Google AdMob Android only

Displays ads after consent is granted (GDPR compliant). Three ad formats are used: banner ads on list pages, rewarded ads gating PDF exports, and frequency-capped interstitial ads shown at most once every three saves across fuel, maintenance, and expense entries.

4

Advertising & Consent

We display ads via Google AdMob on Android only. On first launch, you are presented with a GDPR consent dialog. You may decline personalised ads — the app remains fully functional with non-personalised ads shown instead.

📌
Banner Ads

Small static banners shown at the bottom of list pages (fuel, maintenance, expenses). Non-intrusive and always visible.

🎬
Rewarded Ads

Short video ads shown when you choose to generate a PDF export or Tax Report. Watching the ad unlocks the export. If the ad fails to load, the export proceeds immediately as a fallback — you are never blocked.

⚡
Interstitial Ads

Full-screen ads shown at most once every three saves combined across fuel, maintenance, and expense entries. They appear after the form closes so you are never interrupted mid-task.

5

Data Export & Deletion

You can export all your data at any time from Privacy & Data → Export My Data in JSON, PDF, or Excel format. Each export is gated behind a short rewarded ad; if the ad fails to load the export proceeds immediately. Sensitive fields (VIN, plate number) are masked by default and can be unmasked via the "Export Decrypted Data" toggle in Settings after reviewing the security notice.

To permanently delete all data, go to Privacy & Data → Wipe Data. This deletes the encrypted database and all secure hardware-backed keys from your device. This action is irreversible.

6

Cloud Backup Privacy

If you enable Google Drive backup, your encrypted Isar database is transferred directly between your device and your personal Google Drive appDataFolder. DriveWise AI does not have access to your Google credentials or the contents of the uploaded file.

The backup file is compressed (ZIP), timestamped, and auto-deduplicated so only the latest backup is retained. You control backup frequency (daily / weekly / monthly) from Settings → Backup & Sync.
